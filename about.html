About this project

A lot of people want to read about a topic but don’t know what titles to pick. Book Scout solves that by letting you discover books by subject, then explore details and related ideas.

What problem it solves

Searching for books can be overwhelming if you don’t already know the exact title or author. Subject-based discovery makes it easier to browse and decide what to read next.

What data it uses

Open Library APIs: Subject lists, Work details, and (optionally) a public “My Books” shelf.
